The Cheese Burger

A little about The burger

This burger came to me when I was hungry and had these exact ingredients in my fridge.

If you dont have all the ingredients then use whatever im not picky.

Ingredients

  • Fresh ground beef
  • Bakery burger buns
  • Pickled jalapenos/onions
  • Swiss Cheese
  • Pastrami
  • 1000 islands sauce
  1. Prep sauce mix fresh pickles(and brine), mustard, ketchup and mayo (store in fridge)
  2. Butter or mayo buns then toast
  3. Carmalize onions and jalapenos with butter and salt to season
  4. Cook beef, add pastrami and cheese let cheese melt
  5. Plate, add sauce then meat to bottom bun add onions and jalapenos then top with top bun and more sauce
Mesurements very per serving size so no mesurements listed
