The Cheese Burger

About The burger

This burger came to me when I was hungry and had these exact ingredients in my fridge.

If you don,t have all the ingredients then use whatever don't be picky.

Ingredients

  • Fresh ground beef

  • Bakery burger buns

  • Pickled jalapenos/onions

  • Swiss Cheese

  • Pastrami

  • 1000 islands sauce
  1. step 1: Prep sauce mix, fresh chopped pickles(and a lil brine), mustard, ketchup and mayo (store in fridge)
  2. step 2: Butter or mayo buns then toast
  3. step 3: Caramelize onions and jalapenos with butter and salt to season
  4. step 4: Cook beef, add pastrami and cheese let cheese melt
  5. step 5: Plate, add sauce then meat to bottom bun add onions and jalapenos then top with top bun and more sauce
Measurements very per serving size so no measurements listed
