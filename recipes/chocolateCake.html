Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened coco powder
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 cup vegtable oil
  • i cup milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup of hot (strong) coffee
  1. Step 1: Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour a 9x13-inch baking pan.
  2. Step 2: In a large bowl, stir together flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder, and baking soda. Add oil, milk, eggs, and vanilla; mix until smooth.
  3. Step 3: Stir in hot coffee last. Spread evenly into the prepared pan.
  4. Step 4: Bake in the preheated oven for 25 to 35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the cake comes out clean.
recipe yields 24 servings
